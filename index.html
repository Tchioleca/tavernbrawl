📜 TavernBrawl
Rules of the Brawl
⚔ Objective

Be the last adventurer standing. Reduce your opponents' Life Points to zero while keeping your own above the abyss.

❤️ Life Points

Each player starts with 20 Life Points. When attacked, reduce your HP using the − button. When healed, use the + button. A player whose HP reaches 0 is eliminated from the brawl.

🪙 Coins

Coins are your currency for power. You start with 1 Coin each round. Spend coins to:

  • Purchase items from the Tavern row
  • Hire mercenaries to fight for you
  • Upgrade your Tavern Tier for stronger cards
🍺 The Tavern

Each round, a fresh selection of cards appears in the Tavern. Recruit them to build your board. On your turn, fight a random opponent — the winner deals damage equal to their surviving minions' Attack.

👑 Victory

The last hero with Life Points remaining claims the glory of the Tavern and a round of free ale. May your dice roll true.
